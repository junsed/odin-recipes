Masala Dosa

Description

This classic Masala dosa recipe makes perfectly light, soft and crispy crepes stuffed with a savory, wonderfully spiced potato and onion filling.

It might take a bit of time to make delicious hotel style masala dosa, but with my step-by-step instructions, I promise it will be easier than you think and is so worth the effort!

Ingredients

  1. 1.5 cups idli rice or parboiled rice in one bowl. You can also use regular short-grained to medium-grained rice like sona masuri or parmal.
  2. ½ cup urad dal (husked black gram) + 1 tablespoon chana dal (husked and split bengal gram) + 20 methi seeds (fenugreek seeds) in a second bowl
  3. ⅓ cup thick poha (flattened rice) in a third bowl.

Steps

  1. First take all the ingredients in three bowls. Note that these ingredients are easily available online or any Indian or Asian grocery store if you live outside India.
  2. Rinse the lentils and fenugreek seeds a couple of times. Then soak them in 1 cup water for 4 to 5 hours.
  3. Rinse the rice a couple of times and keep aside.
  4. Rinse flattened rice once or twice and then add to the rice.
  5. Pour 2 cups water. Stir and soak both rice and flattened rice together for 4 to 5 hours.
